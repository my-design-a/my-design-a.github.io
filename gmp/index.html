About Gambhirpur

According to Census 2011 information the location code or village code of Gambhirpur village is 572151.

Gambhirpur village is located in Kathlapur Tehsil of Jagityala district in Telangana, India. Nearest Police Station is located in Kathalapur

It is situated 10km away from sub-district headquarter Kathlapur and 30km away from district headquarter Jagityala.

As per 2009 stats, Gambeepur is the gram panchayat of Gambhirpur village.

The total geographical area of village is 1486 hectares.

Gambhirpur has a total population of 9,000(approx) peoples.

There are about 957 houses in Gambhirpur village.

Koratla is nearest town to Gambhirpur which is approximately 17km away.Nearest Railway Station is Korutla Railway Station
